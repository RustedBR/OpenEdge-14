# Economy & Trading Guide

Master the economy and you'll never need to adventure again. This guide covers currency, jobs, trading, and wealth management.

## Currency System

### Coin Types

OpenEdge 14 uses physical coins with intrinsic value:

**Copper Coin**
- Value: 1 unit
- Color: Reddish-brown
- Common use: Cheap items, tips
- Where found: Everywhere, standard currency

**Silver Coin**
- Value: 10 units (= 10 Copper)
- Color: Shiny gray
- Common use: Medium purchases, equipment
- Where found: Most transactions

**Gold Coin**
- Value: 100 units (= 10 Silver = 100 Copper)
- Color: Bright yellow
- Common use: Expensive equipment, rare items
- Where found: Treasure, major purchases

**Platinum Coin**
- Value: 1000 units (= 10 Gold = 100 Silver)
- Color: Silvery-white with sheen
- Common use: Legendary items, property
- Where found: Rare - treasure chests, high rewards

### Exchange Rates

- 1 Copper = 1 Cu
- 1 Silver = 10 Cu
- 1 Gold = 100 Cu
- 1 Platinum = 1000 Cu

**Example Price List**
- Health Potion: 5 Cu
- Leather Armor: 15 Cu (= 1 Silver + 5 Cu)
- Longsword: 30 Cu (= 3 Silver)
- Full Plate Armor: 100 Cu (= 10 Silver = 1 Gold)
- Magical Staff: 45 Cu (= 4 Silver + 5 Cu)

### Carrying Coins

Coins are items that occupy inventory space:

- **Coin Pouch** (5 coins): Takes 1 inventory slot
- **Coin Sack** (50 coins): Takes 2 inventory slots
- **Coin Chest** (500 coins): Stationary container

**Tip**: Store coins safely in your home or bank to save inventory space!

## Jobs & Salary

### Job System

Every player chooses a job that provides steady income and unique abilities:

**Guard**
- Base Salary: 10 Silver per hour
- Bonus: +5 Silver per criminal arrested
- Benefits: +15% armor durability
- Equipment: Provided (must return)
- Where: Guard Barracks
- Requirements: Strength 12+
- Hours: Day or Night (8-hour shift)

**Merchant**
- Base Salary: 8 Silver per hour
- Bonus: +2 Silver per item sold
- Benefits: Buy items at -20% cost
- Equipment: Shop access
- Where: Market District
- Requirements: Charisma 12+
- Hours: Flexible (Shop hours 6 AM - 10 PM)

**Alchemist**
- Base Salary: 12 Silver per hour
- Bonus: +3 Silver per potion sold
- Benefits: Create potions (-30% material cost)
- Equipment: Lab access
- Where: Alchemist Guild
- Requirements: Intelligence 12+
- Hours: Flexible (Lab open 8 AM - 8 PM)

**Innkeeper**
- Base Salary: 7 Silver per hour
- Bonus: +2 Silver per guest room rented
- Benefits: Free lodging, food discounts (-30%)
- Equipment: Inn access
- Where: Local Inns
- Requirements: Charisma 10+
- Hours: Evening/Night (2 PM - 2 AM)

**Adventurer** (Self-employed)
- Base Salary: 0 (commission-based)
- Income: From looting, bounties, quests
- Benefits: None (complete freedom)
- Equipment: Own equipment
- Where: Adventurer's Guild
- Requirements: Any
- Hours: Any time

**Apprentice** (Training role)
- Base Salary: 3 Silver per hour
- Bonus: +1 Silver per task completed
- Benefits: Learn crafts (-50% training cost)
- Mentor: Assigned by guild
- Where: Various Guilds
- Requirements: None (newbies)
- Hours: Flexible

## Passive Income

### Rental Properties

Own a house and rent it out:

**Small House**
- Purchase: 100 Gold
- Monthly Rent: 5-10 Gold
- Maintenance: 1 Gold per month
- Net Income: 4-9 Gold/month

**Medium House**
- Purchase: 300 Gold
- Monthly Rent: 15-25 Gold
- Maintenance: 2 Gold per month
- Net Income: 13-23 Gold/month

**Mansion**
- Purchase: 1000 Gold
- Monthly Rent: 50-100 Gold
- Maintenance: 5 Gold per month
- Net Income: 45-95 Gold/month

### Farming

Plant crops and sell at market:

**Wheat Farm** (1 tile)
- Cost to setup: 5 Gold
- Growth time: 3 days
- Harvest: 10 Wheat
- Sell value: 1 Gold per unit
- Monthly income: 10 Gold (if harvested 3x)

**Apple Orchard** (5 tiles)
- Cost to setup: 20 Gold
- Growth time: 7 days
- Harvest: 30 Apples
- Sell value: 2 Gold per unit
- Monthly income: 60 Gold

### Commerce & Trading

Buy low, sell high:

**Simple Trade Route**
1. Buy iron ore at 1 Cu per unit (100 Cu total)
2. Sell to smith at 1.5 Cu per unit (150 Cu)
3. Profit: 50 Cu per route
4. Time: 10 minutes
5. Hourly rate: 300 Cu/hour

**Crafting for Profit**
1. Buy leather hide at 2 Cu per unit
2. Craft leather armor (takes 5 hides = 10 Cu cost)
3. Sell at 15 Cu
4. Profit per armor: 5 Cu
5. With fast crafting: 20 Cu/hour

## Merchant Tips

### Pricing Strategy

**Buy Cheap**
- Buy directly from crafters (cheaper than shops)
- Buy in bulk (5% discount)
- Wait for sales (seasonal -20% events)

**Sell High**
- Sell to NPCs who want it most (weapon to guard, potion to mage)
- Sell to shops with high markup
- Demand-driven pricing (rare items = higher price)

**Profit Margins**
- Average markup: 20-30% profit
- Bulk deals: 5-10% profit but faster
- Rare items: 50-100% markup possible

### Market Trends

**High Demand Items** (Easy to sell)
- Health Potions (constant need)
- Iron Weapons (guards want them)
- Leather Armor (starter gear)

**Low Demand Items** (Slow to move)
- Cloth Robes (specialized)
- Blunt Weapons (niche)
- Cheap Potions (no one buys them)

**Seasonal Demand**
- Winter: Heavier armor, warm cloaks
- Summer: Light armor, healing potions
- Harvest: Farm tools, storage
- Festival: Rare items, luxuries

## Banking & Savings

### Bank Accounts

Store coins safely at the bank:

**Free Account**
- Storage: Up to 1000 Gold
- Interest: None
- Deposits: Unlimited
- Withdrawals: Unlimited

**Premium Account**
- Storage: Up to 10000 Gold
- Interest: 1% per month
- Monthly fee: 1 Gold
- Benefits: Insurance against theft

**Vault Account**
- Storage: Unlimited
- Interest: 2% per month
- Monthly fee: 5 Gold
- Benefits: 100% security, emergency loans

**Example**:
With Premium Account at 1% monthly interest:
- Deposit: 1000 Gold
- After 1 month: 1010 Gold earned
- After 1 year: 1127 Gold earned
- Net profit: 127 Gold

## Theft & Crime

### Criminal Economy

Some make money through illegal means:

**Pickpocketing**
- Risk: Caught → 1 day jail + fine
- Reward: 5-50 Cu per successful steal
- Risk/Reward: High risk for small gain

**Robbery**
- Risk: Caught → 3 days jail + large fine
- Reward: 100+ Cu per robbery
- Risk/Reward: Very high risk for good reward

**Counterfeiting**
- Risk: Caught → 1 week jail + losing coins
- Reward: Double your coins (50% success)
- Risk/Reward: Extremely high risk

**Recommendation**: Playing as an honest citizen is more reliable long-term!

## Economic Tips for Wealth

1. **Save Regularly**: Put 10% of earnings into bank
2. **Diversify Income**: Don't rely on one job
3. **Invest Early**: Property compounds over time
4. **Learn Crafts**: Make items to sell for profit
5. **Network**: Build relationships with merchants
6. **Be Patient**: Wealth takes time, don't rush
7. **Track Spending**: Know where money goes
8. **Take Risks**: Some ventures pay big returns
9. **Help Others**: Good reputation = better prices
10. **Enjoy the Ride**: Money isn't everything!

## Common Mistakes

- **Spending Carelessly**: Before you know it, you're broke
- **Trusting Others with Coins**: Storage is your job
- **Avoiding Jobs**: Salary is most reliable income
- **Holding Coins in Inventory**: Use banks and chests
- **Ignoring Property**: Real estate is free money
- **Overpricing Items**: Fair prices build reputation
- **Taking Debt**: Loans are dangerous traps
- **Gambling**: Never a reliable income strategy
