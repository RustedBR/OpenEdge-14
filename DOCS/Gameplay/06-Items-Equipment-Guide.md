## Equipment Management

### Carrying Capacity

Different items have different weights:

- **Very Light**: Daggers, rings, amulets (1 unit each)
- **Light**: Shortbows, shortswords (2 units each)
- **Medium**: Longswords, chainmail (3 units each)
- **Heavy**: Greatswords, full plate (4 units each)

Max carrying capacity: 30 units (can upgrade with training)

### Durability

All equipment degrades with use:

- **High Durability**: Plate armor, greatsword (lasts weeks)
- **Medium Durability**: Leather armor, longsword (lasts days)
- **Low Durability**: Cloth, wooden items (breaks quickly)

Durability is repaired at:
- Smithy (melee weapons)
- Tailor (armor, clothing)
- General Shop (repairs most items)

### Enchanting

Weapons can be enchanted by:

1. **Alchemist**: Add poisons/effects
2. **Mage**: Add magical damage/effects
3. **Enchanter**: Permanent magical enhancement

Enchantment costs: 20-100 Silver depending on effect

## Choosing Your Equipment

### For Tanks (Durability Focus)
- **Armor**: Full Plate
- **Weapon**: Greatsword or War Hammer
- **Shield**: Tower Shield
- **Accessories**: Amulet of Protection

### For DPS (Damage Focus)
- **Armor**: Medium (Chainmail)
- **Weapon**: Battleaxe or Greatsword
- **Shield**: None (two-handed focus)
- **Accessories**: Strength Potion when fighting

### For Rogues (Stealth Focus)
- **Armor**: Leather, light-colored
- **Weapon**: Dagger or Shortsword (dual-wield)
- **Shield**: None (mobility over defense)
- **Accessories**: Boots of Speed

### For Mages (Magic Focus)
- **Armor**: Cloth Robe
- **Weapon**: Magical Staff or Wand
- **Shield**: None
- **Accessories**: Ring of Wisdom, Mana Potions

### For Archers (Ranged Focus)
- **Armor**: Leather Tunic
- **Weapon**: Longbow with arrows
- **Shield**: None
- **Accessories**: Quiver of Arrows, Ring of Dexterity

## Economy Tip

**Smart Shopping**
- Buy armor in bulk at Smithy (5% discount)
- Sell loot to appropriate shops (Smithy for weapons, Tailor for armor)
- Enchant common items for 3x resale value
- Repair damaged items for cheap, resell at profit
